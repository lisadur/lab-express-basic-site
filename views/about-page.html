About the author

Sen Lin Yu is the author of several popular fan fictions which are accessible online for free. The name Sen Lin Yu is a pen name, which translates to "Forest Bathing" or "Forest Rain". The real name of the author is unknown. According to their Instagram profile, Sen Lin Yu identifies as she/they. Their work gained massive popularity in the early 2020s, when many TikTok creators talked about popular works like "Manacled", a fan fiction based on the Harry Potter books by J.K. Rowling.
